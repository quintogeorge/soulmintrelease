# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

## Overview

SoulMint is designed to help users create a generated identity, mint that identity, and experience conversation-based matching. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it may be used, and what user choices are available.

## Information We May Collect

We may collect:

- wallet address and wallet-based authentication data
- profile content you provide, including self-descriptions, preferences, and selected traits
- generated portrait references and related metadata
- chat content and usage activity within the app
- technical information required for app security, analytics, debugging, and service reliability

## How Information May Be Used

We may use information to:

- create and maintain your account and in-app identity
- generate portraits and matching experiences
- power conversation features and usage limits
- process mint-related actions and payment-related checks
- improve product quality, safety, performance, and abuse prevention

## Third-Party Services

SoulMint may rely on third-party services, including infrastructure, authentication, image generation, storage, blockchain, analytics, moderation, and payment-related systems. Data sent to those providers is limited to what is reasonably necessary for the relevant feature.

## Wallets and Blockchain Data

Blockchain activity and wallet addresses may be publicly visible by design. Users are responsible for understanding the public nature of blockchain transactions and wallet-linked records.

## Data Retention

We may retain account, profile, generated portrait, message, and transaction-related records for as long as needed to operate the service, enforce policies, resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, or protect the platform.

## User Controls

Users may be able to:

- update profile details
- delete chats
- delete their account
- disconnect or stop using the app

Some on-chain or blockchain-related records cannot be deleted because of the nature of public ledgers.

## Security

We take reasonable steps to protect service data, but no system can guarantee absolute security. Users should also protect their wallet, device, and account access.

## Children

SoulMint is not intended for children. Users must be legally permitted to use the service in their jurisdiction.

## Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the service after changes means the updated policy may apply.

## Contact

If you operate this repository publicly, replace this section with your preferred support or legal contact details before production launch.
